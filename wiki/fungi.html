üçÑ Fungi

Fungi is a mysterious merchant hidden within the Iceberg Island's maze, offering unique blueprints and potions. Finding Fungi is essential for players looking to upgrade their arsenal.

üìú Fungi Overview

üìç Location: Hidden Maze on Iceberg Island

üîÑ Spawn Logic: Fungi's location within the Secret Maze changes every day.

üéØ Role: Fungi serves as a Merchant (Unique Potions & Weapon Blueprints)

üìñ Description: Fungi is a small merchant who sells a unique mining potion and various powerful weapon blueprints.

üëÄ Fungi's Appearance

The NPC Fungi is a small, light-blue creature with glowing white eyes. Fungi wears a very large blue mushroom cap as a hat, which is decorated with glowing light-blue crystals. He is dressed in blue robes with gold trim and carries a wooden staff topped with a smaller blue mushroom, completing Fungi's signature look.

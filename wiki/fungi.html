üëÄ Fungi's Appearance

The NPC Fungi is a small, light-blue creature with glowing white eyes. Fungi wears a very large blue mushroom cap as a hat, which is decorated with glowing light-blue crystals. He is dressed in blue robes with gold trim and carries a wooden staff topped with a smaller blue mushroom, completing Fungi's signature look.

üí° Tips for Finding Fungi

  • The Hidden Maze on Iceberg Island can be tricky. Fungi moves to a new location within the maze every day.
  • Bring enough Coins! Fungi's blueprints are expensive, ranging from $68,000 to $500,000.
  • Need Coinite? Farm Coinite Ore in the Crimson Depths by mining Red Crystals or Heart of the Island rocks. It's rare, so use a Luck potion if you have one!
  • Fungi's Potion is great for mining sessions, providing a boost to both speed and luck.

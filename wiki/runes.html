üìñ Rune System Overview

üéØ What Are Runes?

Runes are obtainable tokens which let you place special traits on weapons and armor (for example, mining speed buffs). Different runes offer different effects and costs; some are stronger or more expensive to apply than others.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Rules

  • Rune Slots Required: Runes can only be placed on gear that has available rune slots
  • Permanent Enhancement: Once socketed, runes permanently enhance your equipment
  • Secondary Traits: Some runes may have secondary traits that grant extra buffs
  • Stacking Effects: Secondary traits can appear on weapons or armor and stack with the rune's main effect

üîß How to Socket Runes

üéØ Step 1: Find Equipment with Rune Slots

Not all weapons and armor have rune slots. Look for equipment that specifically mentions available rune slots when you craft or find it.

üîÆ Step 2: Obtain Runes

Runes are obtained by defeating specific enemies, completing challenges, or finding them in treasure chests throughout The Forge world.

‚öíÔ∏è Step 3: Visit the Runemaker

Take your equipment and runes to the Runemaker NPC who can socket the runes into your gear for a fee.

üí° Pro Tips for Rune Usage

  • Plan Your Build: Consider which runes complement your playstyle before socketing
  • Rarity Matters: Higher tier runes provide significantly better effects
  • Secondary Traits: Look for runes with beneficial secondary traits for extra value
  • Cost Consideration: Socketing runes costs resources, so choose wisely
  • Permanent Enhancement: Once socketed, runes cannot be removed, so plan carefully

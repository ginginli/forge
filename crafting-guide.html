What is Crafting in The Forge?

Crafting is the core gameplay mechanic in The Forge where you combine ores to create powerful weapons and armor. The crafting system allows you to:

  • Create custom weapons with unique stats
  • Forge armor with specific defensive properties
  • Activate special traits through ore combinations
  • Upgrade existing equipment to higher tiers
  • Experiment with different builds and playstyles

How to Craft: Step-by-Step Guide

1

Gather Ores

Collect the ores you need through mining, codes, or trading. Check our Ore Guide for details.

2

Plan Your Build

Decide what weapon or armor you want to craft and which traits you need for your playstyle.

3

Use Calculator

Input your ore combination into our calculator to preview the results before committing.

4

Access Forge

Go to the Forge station in the game (usually in the main hub or crafting area).

5

Select Item Type

Choose whether you're crafting a weapon (sword, axe, etc.) or armor (helmet, chestplate, etc.).

6

Add Ores

Place your ores into the crafting slots according to your planned combination.

7

Review Stats

Check the preview of your item's stats, modifiers, and active traits before confirming.

8

Craft!

Confirm the craft and receive your new equipment. Ores are consumed permanently.

Warrior Build (Melee Focus)

Goal: High damage and durability

  • Primary Ores: Iron (60%), Shadow (30%), Coal (10%)
  • Weapon Type: Sword or Axe
  • Key Traits: Durability, Sharpness, Critical Hit
  • Armor Focus: High defense with Iron and Coal

Mage Build (Magic Focus)

Goal: Maximum magical power and mana

  • Primary Ores: Crystal (50%), Gem (30%), Gold (20%)
  • Weapon Type: Staff or Wand
  • Key Traits: Magic Power, Mana Regen, Elemental
  • Armor Focus: Mana regeneration with Crystal

Assassin Build (Speed & Crit)

Goal: High critical hits and stealth

  • Primary Ores: Shadow (60%), Iron (25%), Gem (15%)
  • Weapon Type: Dagger or Short Sword
  • Key Traits: Stealth, Critical Hit, Speed
  • Armor Focus: Light armor with Shadow ore

Tank Build (Defense Focus)

Goal: Maximum survivability

  • Primary Ores: Iron (70%), Coal (20%), Gold (10%)
  • Weapon Type: Shield and Mace
  • Key Traits: Durability, Protection, Unbreakable
  • Armor Focus: Heavy armor with maximum Iron

‚ùì Crafting FAQ

Can I undo a craft?

No, crafting is permanent. Once you confirm, the ores are consumed and cannot be recovered. Always use the calculator first!

How many ores do I need for a legendary weapon?

Typically 50-100 total ores, with at least 15-20% being rare ores (Gem or Shadow). Use our calculator for exact amounts.

Can I upgrade crafted items?

Yes! You can add more ores to existing equipment to upgrade it, but this requires additional resources.

What's the best weapon to craft first?

Start with an Iron sword (60% Iron, 30% Coal, 10% Gold) - it's affordable and effective for beginners.

Do ore combinations affect item appearance?

Yes! Different ore combinations can change the visual effects and colors of your equipment.

Can I craft with a friend?

Crafting is individual, but you can trade ores with friends to help each other get the materials needed.

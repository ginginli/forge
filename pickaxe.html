‚õèÔ∏è About Pickaxes in The Forge

üéØ Core Mining Tool

Pickaxes are the Forger's primary tool for breaking Rocks and extracting valuable Ores. Some rocks require higher-tier or specific pickaxes before they can be mined.

  • New Forgers receive a free Stone Pickaxe when they first join the game.
  • There are currently 6 main Pickaxes in The Forge, each scaling in power and utility.
  • Higher-tier pickaxes unlock tougher rocks, faster clears, and better chances at rare ores.

üìä Pickaxe Stats Explained

‚ö° Mine Power

Mine Power is how much damage a pickaxe deals to a rock per swing. Higher-tier pickaxes have more Mine Power and can break tougher rocks much faster.

üçÄ Luck Boost

Luck Boost increases your base luck when mining. Pickaxes with high Luck Boost make it easier to roll rare ores and better drops from rocks.

üíé Rune Slots

Rune Slots determine how many Runes can be socketed into a pickaxe. More slots let you stack powerful mining traits and luck modifiers.

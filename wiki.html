üë§ Races & Traits

Races in The Forge determine a player's innate traits, stat bonuses, and special abilities. Each race has its own strengths and weaknesses.

üìã Race System Overview

  • Total Races: 13 currently available races
  • Rarity Levels: 6 tiers from Common to Mythical
  • Assignment: Randomly assigned when joining the game
  • Rerolling: Players can reroll their race to pursue different builds
  • Obtaining: Join the game or use codes to get race rerolls
  • Benefits: Unique stat bonuses, abilities, and visual appearances
  • Higher Rarity: Better benefits and visuals, but lower roll chances

‚öîÔ∏è Enemies & Combat

Enemies are hostile NPCs that attack players who come near them. Defeat them to earn EXP, Gold, Essences, and other valuable items.

‚ö†Ô∏è Enemy System Overview

  • Hostile NPCs: Enemies attack players who come near them
  • Rewards: Drop EXP, Gold, Essences, and potentially other items
  • Locations: Different enemy types appear in different areas
  • Difficulty: Enemy strength increases in advanced areas
  • Combat: Use weapons and armor to defeat enemies efficiently

üßü Zombies

üìç Location & Background

Zombies are the first set of Enemies you will encounter, located in Stonewake's Cross.

They are most likely the result of an outbreak, which led to the infestation of Zombies present in The Cave and its mines. Zombies roam and guard the area, threatening to attack any brave soul who dares to mine their Ores.

üí° Combat Tips

  • First enemy type - good for beginners to practice combat
  • Found in starter areas, making them accessible early game
  • Guard mining areas, so be prepared when gathering ores
  • Relatively easy to defeat with basic weapons

üíÄ Skeletons

üìç Location & Background

Skeletons are the second set of Enemies you will encounter, located in The Forgotten Kingdom.

They are said to have lived in the sealed mines until the mines were opened up. Shortly after, they escaped the mines and would attack citizens, which resulted in many citizens deserting the kingdom, leaving the place as a former shell of itself.

üí° Combat Tips

  • Second enemy tier - moderate difficulty
  • Found in The Forgotten Kingdom, an intermediate area
  • Stronger than Zombies, requiring better equipment
  • Part of the kingdom's tragic history

üî• Hell Skeletons

üìç Location & Background

There is a variant of Skeletons known as Hell Skeletons, which are the third set of Enemies you will encounter.

Located in the Volcanic depths of The Forgotten Kingdom, they serve as a much stronger and dangerous enemy, requiring you to bring your best Weapons and Armor if you were to brave these creatures straight from the depths of Hell.

‚ö†Ô∏è Combat Tips

  • High Difficulty: Much stronger than regular Skeletons
  • Endgame Content: Requires best weapons and armor
  • Volcanic Location: Found in dangerous volcanic depths
  • Better Rewards: Likely drops better loot and more EXP
  • Fire Resistance: Consider fire-resistant armor

üü¶ Slimes

Slimes are mentioned as an enemy type in The Forge. More information coming soon!

üí° General Combat Tips

  • Progression: Start with Zombies, then Skeletons, then Hell Skeletons
  • Equipment: Upgrade your weapons and armor before facing stronger enemies
  • Rewards: All enemies drop EXP, Gold, and Essences
  • Farming: Defeat enemies repeatedly to gather resources
  • Strategy: Learn enemy attack patterns to fight more efficiently
  • Race Bonuses: Some races like Orc and Minotaur excel in combat
  • Preparation: Stock up on potions from Maria before tough battles
  • Location Awareness: Know which enemies spawn in which areas

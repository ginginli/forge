üéÆ Game Features

Q: Will there be a storyline?

A: Yes, but it won't be a focus.

Q: Will it be available on [Platform]?

A: Yes, it'll be playable on all platforms.

Q: Will the game have gamepasses/p2w?

A: The game will not be p2w with crazy gamepasses like 2x luck but there will be buyables in the form of potions and gamepasses with slight advantages.

Q: Will the game have player levels?

A: Yes, there will be levels that will increase your stash capacity, equipment capacity can be increased with money.

Q: Will there be trading?

A: Yes, there will be trading unlocked in late game.

Q: Will there be runes/how to obtain?

A: Yes, there will be runes, and they can be obtained through mob and boss drops, quests, or achievement rewards.

‚öîÔ∏è Weapons & Combat

Q: How many weapons will there be?

A: Weapons will be separated into different categories with different designs and size.

Q: What weapons are currently in the game?

A: Dagger, gauntlets, straight sword, katana, greatsword, greataxe, and colossal weapons. (The scythe falls under the greataxe category.)

Q: Will there be pvp?

A: Yes, there will be.

Q: What's the game combat like?

A: There's a dash and a block. There are skills planned and passives.

Q: Will there be parry?

A: Yes, the devs are adding parry.

Q: Will we be able to craft magical weapons like staffs?

A: Only swords/melee for now.

Q: Will the game have anime/movie based weapons?

A: There's already a dragonslayer weapon so its safe to assume there will be more fictional weapons.

üõ°Ô∏è Equipment & Customization

Q: Will there be enchants or quality to make equipment unique?

A: Yes, there will be several things that make equipment unique like: quality, ores used and runes.

Q: Will runes only be elemental?

A: No, runes will not only be elemental they'll also have 1-3 traits (you can also enchant gear for more rune slots).

Q: What type of equipment can have runes and how many?

A: Any equipment can have runes as long as you upgrade them, except for pickaxes which some do come with premade rune slots.

Q: What are the armor types now?

A: They are separated into 3 categories: helmet/chestplate/leggings.

üëπ Bosses & Events

Q: Will there be world events?

A: Yes, world events are planned.

Q: Will there be intros to boss fights and cutscenes for deaths?

A: Yes, bosses will have cutscenes/intros as for deaths nothing can be said for that right now.

Q: Do bosses drop materials or loot to craft weapons and stuff?

A: For now they dont but they drop runes currently.

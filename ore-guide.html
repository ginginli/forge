What Are Ores in The Forge?

Ores are the fundamental resources in The Forge used to create weapons and armor. They are obtained by mining different types of rocks found throughout the game world. Each ore has unique characteristics, spawn rates, and can be found in specific rock types. Understanding where to find ores and their rarity is essential for efficient crafting.

üî• Understanding Ore Rarity & Spawn Rates

Ores in The Forge have different rarity levels that affect their spawn rates and usefulness:

Rarity Tiers:

  • Common: Stone (1 in 1), Sand Stone (1 in 2), Copper (1 in 3), Iron (1 in 5), Cardboardite (1 in 31)
  • Uncommon: Tin (1 in 7), Silver (1 in 12), Gold (1 in 16), Bananite (1 in 30)
  • Rare: Mushroomite (1 in 22), Platinum (1 in 28)
  • Epic: Aite (1 in 44), Poopite (1 in 131)

Rock Types & What They Contain:

  • Pebble: Stone, Sand Stone, Copper, Iron, Poopite
  • Rock: Sand Stone, Copper, Iron, Tin, Silver, Mushroomite, Bananite, Cardboardite
  • Boulder: Copper, Iron, Tin, Silver, Gold, Mushroomite, Bananite, Cardboardite, Aite, Poopite
  • Basalt Rock: Silver, Gold, Platinum

‚õèÔ∏è How to Obtain Ores

Mining Rocks:

The primary method to gather ores. Mine different rock types to find various ores:

  • Pebble: Easiest to mine, contains common ores like Stone, Copper, and Iron
  • Rock: Medium difficulty, good variety including Tin, Silver, and Mushroomite
  • Boulder: Harder to mine, best chance for rare ores like Gold, Platinum, and Aite
  • Basalt Rock: Special rock type with Silver, Gold, and Platinum

Ore Trivia:

  • In the game index, Eye Ore is shown as "Eye Ore Ore"
  • Boneite does not appear in the in-game index
  • Cuprite has a green cloud above it in the index

‚ú® Ore Characteristics & Descriptions

Each ore in The Forge has unique characteristics that make it useful for different purposes:

Common Ores - Great for Beginners:

  • Stone: The most basic material - it's literally just a rock
  • Sand Stone: Crumbly but buildable, not water-resistant
  • Copper: Reliable starter material for basic crafting
  • Iron: The workhorse of the forge - versatile and dependable

Uncommon Ores - Better Properties:

  • Tin: Weak alone but excellent for alloy combinations
  • Silver: Pure and malleable with anti-curse properties
  • Gold: Not the strongest but adds style and value
  • Bananite: Surprisingly durable despite its odd appearance

Rare & Epic Ores - Special Properties:

  • Platinum: Smooth, silvery, and highly conductive
  • Mushroomite: Grows in damp places, sometimes glows or talks
  • Cardboardite: Looks like trash but incredibly useful
  • Aite: Made of A's, lightweight and razor sharp
  • Poopite: Smells awful but makes great fertilizer-fused armor
